Welcome to the Richmond Election Comparator!

The Comparator is a tool to help you explore how small areas of Richmond, Virginia, have changed politically over time. By taking a precinct-level look at the change in voting patterns from year to year, you get a much more granular look than if you just see a report about voting outcomes in "the city."

There is also a Context View, which will let you get a little bit more background about the vote each year. All the data is about presidential elections, since that is a consistent data set across precincts, whereas local and state races have much more variety.

Please note that there are situations where there is no data, primarily due to additions and subtractions of precincts over the years. This precinct map is based on that in use for the 2020 presidential election.

Created by Eric Johnson, University of Wisconsin-Madison, August 2024.
